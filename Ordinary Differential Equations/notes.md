**Deriving a Model**

$$ N(t + \Delta{t}) - N(t) = \bar{b} \cdot N(t) - \bar{d} \cdot N(t) $$

$$ b = \frac{\bar{b}}{\Delta{t}}, \quad  d = \frac{\bar{d}}{\Delta{t}}, \quad r = b - d $$

$$ N(t + \Delta{t}) = N(t) + \Delta{t} \cdot r \cdot N(t) $$
